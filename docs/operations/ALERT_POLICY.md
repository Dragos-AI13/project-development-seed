# Alert Policy

## Severity Levels

- P1: user-facing outage or security incident
- P2: major degradation without full outage
- P3: non-critical degradation

## Routing

- P1: immediate notification + incident runbook execution
- P2: same-day remediation plan
- P3: backlog with tracked owner

## Noise Control

- Alerts must be actionable.
- Remove or tune noisy alerts within one sprint.
- Every repeated alert requires root-cause follow-up.
